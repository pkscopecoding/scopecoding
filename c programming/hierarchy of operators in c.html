Hierarchy of Operations

While evaluating an arithmetic statement, some issues may crop up. For example, does the expression 2*x-3*y correspond to (2x)-(3y) ? To Answer this questions , one has to understand the "hierarchy" of operations or The Priority of Operators.

The hierarchy of commonly used operators is shown in Figure below.


Priority Operators
1st * / %
2nd + -
3rd =

Que: According to above Priority, evaluate the following expression, assuming that i is an integer variable:


  • i = 2 * 3 / 4 + 4 / 4 + 8 - 2 + 5 / 8

Ans:
i= 2*3/4+4/4+8-2+5/8
i=6/4+4/4+8-2+5/8
i=1+4/4 +8 -2 +5/8
i=1+1+8-2+5/8
i=1+1+8-2+0
i=2+8-2+0
i=10-2
i=8
